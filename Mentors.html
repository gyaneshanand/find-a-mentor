Mentorship for Engineers! 

I've had engineering students reach out to me for mentorship / finding a good project / problem-solving in general. While I try my best to help them, I frequently find myself either incapable / too busy to help them - and often end up referring them to a more suitable mentor. 

I have compiled a list of good engineers (mostly with CS background) who are more than happy to help their juniors! Most of these are either pursuing higher education, or are corporate-slaves. They are willing to put in some hours to help you grow :)

If you believe you can provide mentorship to engineering students, and are willing to put in a few hours every month - please send me an email to thakker.madhav@gmail.com with your profile picture, resume and social network handle (if any). I'd be more than happy to add you as a mentor. Thanks!
